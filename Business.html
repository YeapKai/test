Business With Us

As 99 Speedmart® continues to expand, we are always searching for opportunities to better meet customer needs. Ranging from goods to our day-to-day operations, there are many avenues of cooperation.

Vendor opportunities include, but are not limited to the following:

Merchandising

We are committed to work with vendors who will help 99 Speedmart® better meet customer requirements, accepting goods from a diverse vendor base to meet expand our range of products. The sample listing form explains our method of procurement and procurement opportunities. Once the form is completed, kindly fax the form to 03-3362 6570.

Download Listing Form

Corporate Supply and Logistics

If you have a product, equipment or a service that can be used to improve our business or meet our daily operational needs, fill up the logistics form below and fax it to us at 03-3362 6579 when completed. Examples of supplies are shown below:

  • Plastic bags
  • Office Supplies (Stationery, tables etc.)
  • Store equipment (Racks, display stands)
  • Vehicle maintenance
Download Logistic Form

Properties and Real Estate

New outlets are opening daily across the Klang Valley and the whole of Malaysia and you can participate in our expansion.

If you have a store available for lease, or multiple locations available for acquisition such as a commercial area or light industry park, we would like to know about it. Fill up the Tenancy Application Form below and fax it to us at 03-3362 6573. The proposed sites should meet the following criterion:

  • Store space requirements of 2,000-5,000 square feet
  • High visibility and excellent accessibility
