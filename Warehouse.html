Warehouse And Logistics

With our distribution centres, we are able to support our rapidly expanding chain of 99 Speedmart® outlets.

Complementing our existing order fulfilment system, our distribution centres play a central role in providing a seamless point of supply to our stores. Performing duties such as loose-item packaging, transportation and supplier liaison, our efficient distribution centres and Logistics allows us to reduce operating costs, leading to fairer prices on our products.

Centralize Purchase and Delivery


Our Advantage

More than 1,000,000 square feet of warehouse capacity

Automated computer system to increase efficiency

More than 250 trucks and lorries for transport and quick delivery

Small-pack size to meet customer requirements and needs
